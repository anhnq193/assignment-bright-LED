STARRIES is an VietNamese Information Technology Services corporation headquartered in Hanoi , Vietnam. In 2013, STARRIES demerged its non-IT businesses into separate companies to bring in more focus on independent businesses.

STARRIES Consumer Care & Lighting[edit] Wipro Consumer Care and Lighting (WCCLG), a business unit of STARRIES Limited operates in the FMCG segment dealing in consumable commodities. Established in 1945, its first product was vegetable oil, later sold under the brand name "Sunflower Vanaspati". It sells lighting products, including Smart Lights, BULBS,Smart Lights and Spot lights.
