About us

Deep learn what we do

Welcome to Starries, your number one source for all things LED lights. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of LED lights, with a focus on dependability, customer service and uniqueness.

Founded in 2017 by 2G2B, Staris has come a long way from its beginnings in a home office. When 2G2B first started out, their passion for helping other parents be more eco-friendly drove him to do intense research, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over Vietnam, and are thrilled to be a part of the eco-friendly wing of the light industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Sincerely,

2G2B co-Fouder
